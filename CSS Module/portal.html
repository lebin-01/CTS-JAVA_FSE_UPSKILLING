Community Bulletin

The CivicPulse Gazette

Council Updates & Local News

The city council is pleased to announce the launch of the expanded Community Events Programme for the summer season. With over 48 events planned across 12 venues, this is the most ambitious schedule the council has ever undertaken for residents.

New facilities at the Riverside Trail have been completed ahead of schedule, allowing the Green City Nature Walk to proceed on July 26th. The trail now features improved accessibility ramps and informational signage at key ecological points.

The Cultural Food Fair in August will feature over 30 stalls representing cuisines from across the globe. Local restaurants and home chefs are encouraged to apply for a stall permit before July 15th via the council website.

Community Hall B has been refurbished and is now equipped with modern audio-visual equipment, making it suitable for workshops, screenings, and small performances. Residents may book the hall through the portal at a subsidised rate.

The council is also launching a new Youth Ambassador Programme this season, inviting residents aged 16–25 to co-design future events. Applications open August 1st and close August 31st. Selected ambassadors will serve a six-month term.

Parking arrangements near Central Park will be modified during the Music Festival. Residents are advised to use the shuttle service from the eastern car park. Full details are available in the event FAQ on this portal.

"Events like these remind us that community is not just a place — it's a feeling."

Task 11

Debugging with Chrome DevTools

Use these techniques to inspect, debug, and refine the portal's CSS live.

Inspect Element

Right-click any element → Inspect. Edit styles in the Styles panel live. Toggle declarations on/off to test changes.

Device Toolbar

Press Ctrl+Shift+M to simulate phone and tablet screens. Watch the breakpoint indicator (bottom-right) change.

Network Tab

Open DevTools → Network → refresh. Confirm styles.css loads with status 200.

Box Model Panel

Select any .eventCard → look at the box model diagram in the Computed tab. Hover each layer to highlight it.

Debug Layout Mode

In Console, type: document.body.classList.toggle('debug-layout') to outline every element on the page.

Force Link States

In Styles panel, click the :hov button and check :hover, :visited, or :active to test link pseudo-classes without clicking.
